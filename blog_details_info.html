What is ChatGPT ?

10 FEB 2023

Al tool ChatGPT gained one million users just five days after launching in November of last year. The conversational Al bot that can produce human-like text has been put to all kinds of uses, from writing short stories, prose, music and term papers to programming basic code, solving math problems and doing translations.

Microsoft founder Bill Gates has already warned that Al technology like ChatGPT could be replacing people in white collar jobs. Microsoft yesterday confirmed a multimillion dollar investment in U.S. company OpenAI, the makers of ChatGPT.

Opinions disagreed and multiplied as we found people supporting the idea that AI would be able to replace man one day and others denying this hypothesis and saying it was not possible or impossible.

AI is seen as a tool that can greatly improve our lives by solving complex problems and making many tasks easier and more efficient. Some believe that AI has the potential to drive innovation and economic growth, leading to new job opportunities and an overall better standard of living.

Some are concerned that AI will lead to mass unemployment as machines replace human workers in many industries. They also worry about the potential for AI to be used for malicious purposes, such as in autonomous weapons or for mass surveillances.

Some are skeptical about the hype surrounding AI, and believe that many of its touted benefits are overstated. They argue that AI is still far from being able to fully replace human intelligence, and that many of its limitations and biases have yet to be addressed.
